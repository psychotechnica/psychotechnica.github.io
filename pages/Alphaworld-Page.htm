Temple of Light
Light Temple Structure
Many of the cultures who have populated this continent provide clues to their perspectives on life by the prominent structures they have left behind. This "Temple" appears to have been built by a branch of the NeoTechnics, characterized by their love of circularity and openness in architecture, while also engaging in many "underground" connections that serve to link their far-flung outposts. Ocassionally, these passages lead to small intersections with the builds of other, more primitive cultures, in ways that are subtle and hidden. It seem they were designed to allow these NeoTechnics to "observe" or study the actions and movements of other contemporary cultures, while remaining largely unobserved themselves. What was their role in the history of this continent?

Psychotechnic Research Center
The Psychotechnic Research Center
This facility provides evidence of a culture far advanced of those populating the main continent. Situated offshore, it is divided into six large rings surrounding a major multi-ring structure and core control facility. The six remote sections each house "experiments" that reveal technologies little understood today. In particular, there is evidence of certain space/time distortion experiments. One is cautioned to proceed carefully in these areas, as one may get caught in an infinite regress warp.
