Unota Unota (a Universal Notation) presents a 3-Dimensional langauge that may be read in varied directions. By plying some 60 verbs, represented by directable picto-glyphs, with 15 relational or directional postures, one obtains over 900 "prepositional verb" expression-symbols. Examples include "to carry across" (trans port) and "to throw out" (e ject), each of which can be inscribed in a circle or rectangle to form the corresponding noun (transportation, ejection).
